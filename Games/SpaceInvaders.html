Game Instruction

Cosmic Clash is a classic arcade game in which you need to shoot down all the aliens. This HTML5 version is inspired by the game from Taito.

How to Play

  • On desktop: Press left and right arrows key to move ship. Press Spacebar to shoot bullets.
  • On mobile: Press left and right on the directional pad to move ship. Press the red button to shoot bullets.
